ABOUT VDK SPORTSCLUB RUN


INTRODUCTION

VDK club are strictly voluntary and involvement in sport and enhance the student's college experience and contribute to the student's overall education and wellness.
All programmes are based on student interest and designed to enhance the dickinson experience by creating an environment where students can unite in diverse group to achieve common goals and objectives while encouraging healthy lifestyles.
The Clubs with the most success and stability are those with active members and dedicated officers.

COMPOSITION

A representive from each recognised sports club, who is in good academic and social standing. These members having voting rights and may represent their club on the sports club. The Director of Intramurals and Recreation who will not have voting rights.

RESPONSIBILITIES OF CLUB
